Project Idea


  • Overview (100 words)
    • This project aims to create an audio plugin software to assist in digital music production, songwriting, and composition. The software will generate possible suitable chord progressions and / or melodies to be used in any music project, based on inputs from the user such as song key, song tempo, desired emotion, degree of complexity. The plugin will output the chosen information as MIDI files. Users will also be able to preview the output of the plugin as audio before exporting. The MIDI files which will be generated by this plugin can be used to trigger sounds from any synthesiser, regardless of whether software or hardware.
  • Motivation (100 words)
    • The music industry generates close to 2 billion dollars in revenue annually for songwriters, composers and producers alone. Due to the rapid increase in processing power of personal computers in the early 2000s, the majority of music makers shifted their working process from bulky, expensive and unreliable hardware to cheap, accurate and reliable software. As someone who has personally been making music on computers professionally for the past 10+ years , I’m acutely aware of amount of human labour which is needlessly repeated on tasks which could easily be automated. I intend to use my experience as a writer, in conjunction with my gradually improving skills as a programmer, to help others increase their efficiency, productivity, and possibly even creativity.
  • Description (500 words)
    • This software’s primary function is to enable users with little to no musical knowledge to effortlessly build detailed and emotive musical compositions with the click of a few buttons and the turning of a few knobs.
